# DCSS Items & Equipment Reference

This guide provides a comprehensive reference for items and equipment in Dungeon Crawl Stone Soup (DCSS), including weapons, armor, jewelry, and consumables.

## Weapons

Weapons are categorized by their skill type. Each weapon has a base damage, accuracy, and speed (delay).

### Melee Weapons
- **Short Blades**: Fast, low damage, excellent for stabbing (Dagger, Short Sword, Rapier).
- **Long Blades**: Balanced speed and damage, can riposte (Scimitar, Long Sword, Great Sword).
- **Axes**: Can hit multiple enemies at once (Hand Axe, War Axe, Broad Axe, Battleaxe).
- **Maces & Flails**: High damage, simple to use (Mace, Flail, Morningstar, Great Mace).
- **Polearms**: Can reach enemies 2 tiles away (Spear, Trident, Halberd, Glaive).
- **Staves**: Magical enhancers that also serve as weapons (Quarterstaff, Lajatang).
- **Unarmed Combat**: Scales with skill, very powerful at high levels.

### Ranged Weapons
- **Bows**: Fast, good range (Shortbow, Longbow).
- **Crossbows**: Slower but high damage and accuracy (Arbalest, Triple Crossbow).
- **Slings**: Fast, can be used with a shield (Hunting Sling, Greatsling).
- **Throwing**: Consumable weapons (Darts, Javelins, Boomerangs, Large Rocks).

## Armor

Armor provides AC (Armor Class) but can penalize your evasion (EV) and spellcasting success.

### Armor Types
- **Robes**: No AC, no penalties.
- **Light Armor**: Low AC, low penalties (Leather Armor, Steam Dragon Scales).
- **Medium Armor**: Balanced AC and penalties (Ring Mail, Scale Mail, Swamp Dragon Scales).
- **Heavy Armor**: High AC, high penalties (Chain Mail, Plate Armor, Crystal Plate Armor).

### Auxiliary Armor
- **Cloaks**: Provide small AC bonus.
- **Gloves**: Provide small AC bonus.
- **Boots**: Provide small AC bonus.
- **Helmets/Hats**: Provide small AC bonus.
- **Shields**: Block attacks (Buckler, Kite Shield, Tower Shield).

---

## Jewelry

Jewelry provides powerful passive bonuses and resistances.

### Rings
- **Protection**: +AC.
- **Evasion**: +EV.
- **Strength/Intelligence/Dexterity**: +Stats.
- **Fire/Cold/Poison Resistance**: Elemental protection.
- **Slaying**: +Damage and accuracy.
- **Wizardry**: +Spell success.

### Amulets
- **Regeneration**: Faster HP recovery.
- **Mana Regeneration**: Faster MP recovery.
- **Reflection**: Reflect projectiles.
- **Guardian Spirit**: Damage taken from MP before HP.
- **Acrobat**: +EV while moving.

---

## Consumables

### Potions
- **Healing**: Restore HP.
- **Magic**: Restore MP.
- **Might**: +Strength and melee damage.
- **Haste**: Increased speed.
- **Invisibility**: Become invisible.
- **Curing**: Cure poison, confusion, and blindness.

### Scrolls
- **Identify**: Reveal item properties.
- **Remove Curse**: Uncurse equipment.
- **Teleportation**: Random teleport after a short delay.
- **Blinking**: Instant short-range teleport.
- **Fear**: Cause nearby enemies to flee.
- **Acquirement**: Request a powerful item from the game.

---

## Artifacts

Artifacts are unique items with fixed or random properties.
- **Fixed Artifacts (Unrands)**: Unique items with specific names and lore (e.g., The Singing Sword).
- **Random Artifacts (Randarts)**: Procedurally generated items with multiple properties.

---

## Item Brands (Egos)

Brands add special effects to weapons and armor.
- **Flaming/Freezing**: Elemental damage.
- **Venom**: Poison on hit.
- **Holy Wrath**: Extra damage to undead and demons.
- **Vampiric**: Heal on hit.
- **Speed**: Faster attacks.
- **Protection (Armor)**: Extra AC.
- **Resistance (Armor)**: Elemental resistance.
