## Overview

**Total Classes**: 34

Tiny Rogues features 34 distinct character classes divided into three rosters:
- **Roster 1**: 17 starting classes (unlocked through early progression)
- **Roster 2**: 17 advanced classes (unlocked via throne ascensions and guardian defeats)
- **Special**: 1 seasonal class (Santa)

Each class has unique base stats, innate abilities, and starting equipment that define their playstyle and synergies with specific [items](items.md) and builds.

### Stat Abbreviations

| Abbreviation | Full Name | Effect |
|--------------|-----------|--------|
| **HP** | Heart Containers | Maximum health |
| **SH** | Soul Hearts | Blue hearts (extra life buffer) |
| **AP** | Armor Points | Damage reduction |
| **MP** | Mana Points | Spell/ability resource |
| **SP** | Stamina Points | Dash charges |
| **STR** | Strength | Melee damage scaling |
| **DEX** | Dexterity | Ranged/speed scaling |
| **INT** | Intelligence | Magic damage scaling |
| **CRIT%** | Critical Hit Chance | Percentage chance for critical hits |
| **CRITX** | Critical Multiplier | Damage multiplier on crits (e.g., 200 = 2.0x) |
| **SPEED** | Movement Speed | Base movement speed |

## Archetype Distribution

| Archetype | Count | Classes |
|-----------|-------|---------|
| **Melee** | 10 | Knight, Warrior, Soldier, Barbarian, Paladin, Monk, Samurai, Dragoon, Dancer, Jester |
| **Ranged** | 4 | Ranger, Gunslinger, Demon Hunter, Ninja (hybrid) |
| **Magic** | 7 | Sorcerer, Wizard, Pyromancer, Alchemist, Necromancer, Druid, Esper |
| **Hybrid** | 9 | Cleric, Mystic, Thief, Bandit, Bard, Pirate, Cyborg, Dragoon, Samurai |
| **Support** | 2 | Cleric, Bard |
| **Balanced** | 2 | The Hero, Super Hero |
| **Variable** | 2 | Doppelganger, Chaos |
| **Seasonal** | 1 | Santa |

### Archetype Characteristics

**Melee**: High STR, close-range combat, synergizes with melee weapons and armor
**Ranged**: High DEX, distance attacks, synergizes with bows/guns and far-range damage
**Magic**: High INT, spell-based damage, synergizes with wands/staves and elemental effects
**Hybrid**: Balanced stats, flexible playstyles, synergizes with diverse equipment
**Support**: Utility abilities, team/buff focus, synergizes with companions and auras
**Balanced**: Equal stats (2/2/2), versatile, synergizes with all builds
**Variable**: Randomized or changeable mechanics, unpredictable gameplay

## Unlock Progression Paths

### Throne Ascensions Required

**Celestial Throne** (Holy/Light path):
- Alchemist (from Wizard)
- Druid (from Pyromancer)
- Monk (from Cleric)
- Paladin (from Knight)
- Super Hero (from The Hero)

**Infernal Throne** (Dark/Chaos path):
- Barbarian (from Warrior)
- Demon Hunter (from Ranger)
- Jester (from Bard)
- Ninja (from Thief)

**Primordial Throne** (Ancient/Transcendent path):
- Chaos (from Doppelganger)
- Esper (from Deprived)
- Samurai (from Wanderer)

### Guardian Defeats Required

**Guardian of High Heavens**:
- Cyborg (from Gunslinger)
- Dancer (from Mystic)

**Guardian of Burning Hells**:
- Dragoon (from Soldier)
- Pirate (from Bandit)

**Guardian of Shadow Planes**:
- Necromancer (from Sorcerer)

### Recommended Unlock Order

**Early Game** (Floors 1-3):
1. Knight, Ranger, Sorcerer (floor 1)
2. Cleric (floor 2)
3. Warrior (floor 3)

**Mid Game** (Progression Challenges):
4. Pyromancer (floor 7)
5. Bandit (750 gold)
6. Bard (tavern visits)
7. Mystic (4 charms)
8. Thief (50 keys)
9. Gunslinger (gun upgrade)
10. Soldier (polearm upgrade)

**Late Game** (Death Defeats):
11. Wanderer (defeat Death)
12. Wizard (40 INT)
13. Deprived (Death + 5 curses)
