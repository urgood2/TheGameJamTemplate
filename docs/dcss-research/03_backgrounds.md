# DCSS Backgrounds Guide

This comprehensive guide covers all backgrounds (classes) in Dungeon Crawl Stone Soup, including starting skills, equipment, progressions, and optimal strategies.

## Background Overview

Backgrounds determine your character's initial skills, equipment, and stat distribution. They provide the foundation for your character build but don't restrict long-term development.

## Melee Backgrounds

### Fighter
**Starting Skills**: Fighting 3, Weapon Skill 2, Armour 3, Shields 3
**Initial Equipment**: +0 weapon of choice, +0 scale mail, +0 buckler, 2 potions of might
**Stat Bonuses**: +8 STR, +4 DEX
**Recommended Species**: Mountain Dwarf, Minotaur, Gargoyle, Troll
**Playstyle**: Versatile warrior with good defensive foundation
**Progression Path**: Build weapon skill to 1.0 delay early, branch into magic or continue pure melee
**Strengths**: Excellent starting equipment, flexible progression
**Limitations**: Generic start, may feel boring initially

### Gladiator
**Starting Skills**: Fighting 2, Weapon Skill 3, Throwing 2, Dodging 3
**Initial Equipment**: +0 weapon, +0 leather armor, +0 helmet, 3 throwing nets, javelin
**Stat Bonuses**: +6 STR, +6 DEX
**Recommended Species**: Hill Orc, Troll, Merfolk, Gargoyle
**Playstyle**: Tactical fighter with crowd control options
**Progression Path**: Master throwing nets, build chosen weapon skill
**Strengths**: Versatile combat options, good mobility
**Limitations**: Lighter armor, limited ammo

### Berserker
**Starting Skills**: Fighting 3, Weapon Skill 3, Armour 2, Dodging 2
**Initial Equipment**: +0 weapon, +0 animal skin
**Stat Bonuses**: +9 STR, +4 DEX, -1 INT
**Recommended Species**: Mountain Dwarf, Minotaur, Merfolk, Gargoyle
**Playstyle**: Savage warrior with Trog worship, berserk ability
**Progression Path**: Use berserk strategically, build melee skills
**Strengths**: Extremely powerful early game, Trog's powers
**Limitations**: No magic, rage management required

### Monk
**Starting Skills**: Fighting 3, Weapon Skill 3, Dodging 3
**Initial Equipment**: +0 weapon, +0 robe, orb of light, potion of ambrosia
**Stat Bonuses**: +3 STR, +2 INT, +7 DEX
**Recommended Species**: Mountain Dwarf, Merfolk, Gargoyle, Troll
**Playstyle**: Ascetic warrior with divine focus potential
**Progression Path**: Convert to god quickly, use orb effectively
**Strengths**: Piety boost on conversion, orb utility
**Limitations**: Weak starting equipment

## Background Selection Strategy

### For Beginners
1. **Fighter**: Balanced progression, forgiving early game
2. **Berserker**: Powerful early game, straightforward tactics
3. **Hunter**: Ranged safety, learning combat positioning

### For Spellcasters
1. **Elementalist**: Focus on single element for efficiency
2. **Summoner**: Safe playstyle with minion shields
3. **Necromancer**: Powerful undead army potential

### For Melee Specialists
1. **Berserker**: Highest damage output
2. **Fighter**: Most reliable progression
3. **Gladiator**: Tactical options with nets

### For Hybrid Builds
1. **Warper**: Mobility + melee combination
2. **Enchanter**: Stealth + magic synergy
3. **Artificer**: Item-based versatility

## Background-Specific Tips

### Fighter
- Upgrade weapon skill first for faster combat
- Consider early god conversion for piety boost
- Use potions of might strategically

### Berserker
- Berserk immediately in dangerous fights
- Lure enemies to clear areas before berserking
- Remember Trog's Hand for magic resistance

### Hunter
- Conserve ammunition with careful aiming
- Use stairs for escape routes
- Consider upgrading to better ranged weapons

### Elementalist
- Focus on one element school first
- Manage spell hunger with food
- Learn complementary spells for weaknesses

### Warper
- Use blink for escape and positioning
- Learn controlled blink for tactical movement
- Combine translocations with melee tactics

### Enchanter
- Use invisibility for safe stabbing
- Master hex spells for enemy control
- Combine with short blades for backstabs
