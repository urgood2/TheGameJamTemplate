# Vampire Survivors: Evolutions Reference

## Overview

Weapon evolutions are the primary power spikes in Vampire Survivors. Evolving a weapon dramatically increases its effectiveness and often adds new mechanics.

---

## Evolution Requirements

### Standard Evolution Process
1. **Max Level Weapon**: Upgrade weapon to Level 8 (or max)
2. **Passive Item**: Have the corresponding passive item (any level)
3. **Treasure Chest**: Open a chest dropped by a boss (10+ minutes)

### Advanced Evolution Process
1. **Max Level Weapon**: Upgrade weapon to Level 8
2. **Max Level Passive(s)**: Specific passives must be at Level 5
3. **Treasure Chest**: Open a chest dropped by a boss

### Union Process
1. **Both Weapons Max**: Both weapons at Level 8
2. **Passive (if required)**: Some unions need a passive
3. **Treasure Chest**: Open a chest

## Evolution Priority Guide

### First Evolution (10-15 min)
**Recommended**: Whip → Bloody Tear
- Easy to obtain (Hollow Heart is common)
- Provides sustain for rest of run
- Whip is a starting weapon for Antonio

### Second Evolution (15-20 min)
**Recommended**: King Bible → Unholy Vespers
- Permanent protection
- Spellbinder is useful for other weapons too
- Great defensive option

### Third Evolution (20-25 min)
**Recommended**: Fire Wand → Hellfire OR Axe → Death Spiral
- Both provide massive damage
- Death Spiral better for crowds
- Hellfire better for bosses

### Late Game Evolutions (25+ min)
**Recommended**: Infinite Corridor (if possible)
- Requires hidden items (Yellow Sign)
- Halves all enemy HP
- Game-changing power spike
