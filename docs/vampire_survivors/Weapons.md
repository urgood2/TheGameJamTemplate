# Vampire Survivors: Weapons Reference

## Overview

Vampire Survivors features **100+ weapons** that auto-attack based on cooldown timers. Weapons level from 1-8 (some to 12), gaining stats at each level. At max level, weapons can evolve with the correct passive item.

---

## Weapon Stats Explained

| Stat | Description | Affected By |
|------|-------------|-------------|
| **Base Damage** | Damage per hit | Might |
| **Area** | Size of attack hitbox | Area stat, Candelabrador |
| **Speed** | Projectile velocity | Speed stat, Bracer |
| **Amount** | Projectiles per attack | Amount stat, Duplicator |
| **Duration** | How long effects last | Duration stat, Spellbinder |
| **Cooldown** | Time between attacks | Cooldown stat, Empty Tome |
| **Pierce** | Enemies hit before expiring | Weapon-specific |
| **Knockback** | Enemy pushback on hit | Weapon-specific |
| **Crit Chance** | Critical hit probability | Luck, Arcanas |
| **Crit Multiplier** | Critical hit damage bonus | Arcanas |

## Weapon Tier List

### S-Tier (Build-Defining)
- **Bloody Tear** - Sustain + damage
- **Unholy Vespers** - Permanent protection
- **Death Spiral** - Massive AoE clear
- **Phieraggi** - Insane DPS with revivals
- **Infinite Corridor** - Halves all enemy HP

### A-Tier (Excellent)
- **Hellfire** - High single-target damage
- **La Borra** - Great zone control
- **Heaven Sword** - Auto-crit damage
- **Thunder Loop** - Good AoE coverage
- **Vandalier** - Solid bombardment

### B-Tier (Good)
- **Holy Wand** - Consistent damage
- **Thousand Edge** - Good directional clear
- **Soul Eater** - Sustain option
- **NO FUTURE** - Bouncing chaos
- **Mannajja** - Vertical clear

### C-Tier (Situational)
- **Gorgeous Moon** - Screen clear utility
- **Vicious Hunger** - Gold farming
- **Valkyrie Turner** - Movement-based
- **Crimson Shroud** - Defensive option

## Weapon Synergies

### High Damage Combo
- Fire Wand + Spinach → Hellfire
- Axe + Candelabrador → Death Spiral
- Cross + Clover → Heaven Sword
- **Result**: Massive burst damage

### Survival Combo
- Whip + Hollow Heart → Bloody Tear
- Garlic + Pummarola → Soul Eater
- Laurel + Metaglios → Crimson Shroud
- **Result**: High sustain, hard to kill

### Screen Clear Combo
- King Bible + Spellbinder → Unholy Vespers
- Santa Water + Attractorb → La Borra
- Lightning Ring + Duplicator → Thunder Loop
- **Result**: Full screen coverage

### Projectile Spam Combo
- Knife + Bracer → Thousand Edge
- Magic Wand + Empty Tome → Holy Wand
- Runetracer + Armor → NO FUTURE
- **Result**: Constant projectile barrage
