# Wands — Complete Catalog (POA Ultrawork Style)

> Primary source: [Magicraft Wiki](https://magicraft.fandom.com/wiki/Wands). All wands listed; includes stats and passives. Wands define MP pool, regen, cast interval, cooldown (CD), scatter, simultaneous firing, and post-slot charging. Casting order is left→right unless reversed by specific wands.

---

## Executive Snapshot
- **Engines**: Per-wand mana; swap wands to recharge. Cast Interval = per-slot delay; CD = end-of-sequence downtime.
- **Archetypes**: Machine Gun (low interval, low MP), Nuke (high MP/cost, slower), Utility (simultaneous fire, reverse, post-slot chargers), Mobility (movement buffs).
- **Design insight**: Post-slots are passive/charge channels—never leave empty. Simultaneous fire wands (Harmonic Resonance, Stellar Drift) are volley enablers; reverse wands (Defiant Nature) invert ordering.

## Wand Archetypes
| Archetype | Example Wands | Characteristics | Use Cases |
| :-- | :-- | :-- | :-- |
| Machine Gun | Featherweight, Swiftcaster, Ripple, Shattered Scepter | Very low interval, modest MP | Poison stacking, mana batteries |
| Nuke / Payload | Stellar Drift, Star Strider, Wand of Focus, Omen of Doom | High MP, simultaneous slots, high cost | Volley payloads (Nova/Meteor) |
| Utility / Sustain | Microcosmic Embrace, Natural Convergence, Sorcerer's Oath, Shard of Time | Global MP cost/recovery buffs | Supports multi-wand rotations |
| Reverse / Special | Defiant Nature (reverse order), Restless Heart (reversed output), Mimic/color wands (Tricolor, Radiant Bouquet) | Order tricks, random color effects | Build-around chains |
| Post-Slot Chargers | 777, Frenzy Enchanter, Rusty Blaster, Book of Tranquility, Conch Whistle, Vow of Honor | Charge-based secondary fire | Passive bursts while main wand works |
| Mobility | Witch's Broomstick, Swift Spellblade, Conch Whistle | Movement/flying bonuses | Kiting, trap avoidance |

## Design Insights
- **Recharge vs Cast Interval**: Low interval with high CD (e.g., Harmonic Resonance) favors burst-volley; high interval with low CD favors sustained fire.
- **Scatter management**: Volley wands with high scatter require Track/Homing; otherwise discounts are wasted.
- **Simultaneous slots**: Always cluster payloads and include homing/area modifiers to realize the discount benefit.
- **Post-slot economy**: Treat post-slot as a passive damage/mana engine—pair charge-per-hit with multihit spells for constant procs.

---

## Implementation Notes
- Model wands with: `mp_max`, `mp_regen`, `cast_interval`, `cooldown`, `scatter`, `simul_count`, `post_slot_trigger(action, gain)`, `flags` (reverse, color_random, cost_multiplier).
- Reverse casting (Defiant Nature) should invert slot traversal; simultaneous fire executes in parallel; post-slot triggers accumulate independently of main cast loop.
