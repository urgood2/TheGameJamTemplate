## Detailed Species Breakdown

### Human
**Innate Traits**: Standard stat progression, no special abilities or restrictions
**Aptitudes**: Balanced across all skills at 100% (baseline)
**Strengths**: Versatile, no equipment restrictions, compatible with all backgrounds
**Weaknesses**: No special advantages, average stat modifiers
**Recommended Backgrounds**: Fighter, Wizard, any hybrid build
**Progression**: Standard XP and stat gains

### Deep Elf
**Innate Traits**: High intelligence aptitudes (+30% magic), fragile HP
**Aptitudes**: Magic schools at 120-140%, fighting skills at 70-80%
**Strengths**: Excellent spellcasters, good magic resistance
**Weaknesses**: Low HP, poor with heavy armor/weapons
**Recommended Backgrounds**: Elementalist, Conjurer, Summoner
**Progression**: Fast XL advancement due to XP mod

### Mountain Dwarf
**Innate Traits**: High HP/STR, slow movement, good with armor
**Aptitudes**: Fighting skills 120%, magic schools 60-70%
**Strengths**: Excellent tanks, good with heavy equipment
**Weaknesses**: Poor spellcasters, slow exploration speed
**Recommended Backgrounds**: Fighter, Berserker, Gladiator
**Progression**: Slow XL advancement but high survivability

### Troll
**Innate Traits**: Extreme STR/HP, claws, regeneration, poor intelligence
**Aptitudes**: Fighting 140%, magic 30-40%, unarmed 150%
**Strengths**: Highest melee damage, regeneration, no weapon needed
**Weaknesses**: Cannot use most armor, terrible magic, XP penalty
**Recommended Backgrounds**: Berserker (Trog), Fighter
**Progression**: Very slow XL due to -40% XP mod

### Spriggan
**Innate Traits**: Extremely fast, no equipment, fragile, herbivorous
**Aptitudes**: Stealth 150%, Dodging 130%, fighting 80%
**Strengths**: Unmatched speed, excellent stealth, fast exploration
**Weaknesses**: Cannot wear armor, very low HP, food restrictions
**Recommended Backgrounds**: Enchanter, Assassin, Transmuter
**Progression**: Fastest XL advancement due to speed advantage

### Minotaur
**Innate Traits**: Horns attack, high STR/HP, good fighting aptitudes
**Aptitudes**: Fighting 130%, axes 140%, magic 60-70%
**Strengths**: Excellent melee damage, horns attack, sturdy
**Weaknesses**: Poor spellcasters, mediocre dodging
**Recommended Backgrounds**: Fighter, Berserker, Gladiator
**Progression**: Standard advancement with good survivability

### Naga
**Innate Traits**: Amphibious, snake body, constriction attack, slow movement
**Aptitudes**: Unarmed 120%, polearms 110%, ice magic 80%
**Strengths**: Water access, constriction, good HP
**Weaknesses**: Very slow on land, poor with boots/shields
**Recommended Backgrounds**: Transmuter, Enchanter, Ice Elementalist
**Progression**: Slower advancement due to movement penalties

### Demonspawn
**Innate Traits**: Random mutations at levels, balanced stats, chaotic alignment
**Aptitudes**: Variable based on mutations, typically balanced
**Strengths**: Unique abilities, unpredictable advantages
**Weaknesses**: Unreliable progression, chaotic mutations can be detrimental
**Recommended Backgrounds**: Chaos Knight, Abyssal Knight, any class
**Progression**: Standard with mutation-based improvements

### Felid
**Innate Traits**: No hands, claws, very fast, 9 lives, small size
**Aptitudes**: Dodging 150%, stealth 140%, unarmed 120%
**Strengths**: Speed, stealth, 9 extra lives, claws
**Weaknesses**: No equipment, extremely low HP, no item use
**Recommended Backgrounds**: Enchanter, Assassin, Monk
**Progression**: Very fast XL with high risk/reward

### Draconians
**Innate Traits**: Scales, breath weapon, color-based abilities, flight at XL 7
**Aptitudes**: Fighting 110%, magic 80-90% (varies by color)
**Strengths**: Breath weapons, natural armor, flight, resistances
**Weaknesses**: Variable abilities based on color, moderate aptitudes
**Subtypes**:
- **Black Draconian**: Negative energy breath, good resistances
- **White Draconian**: Cold breath, ice resistance
- **Red Draconian**: Fire breath, fire resistance
- **Green Draconian**: Acid breath, poison resistance
- **Yellow Draconian**: Lightning breath, electricity resistance
- **Grey Draconian**: Balanced abilities
- **Purple Draconian**: Metabolic breath, unique abilities
- **Mottled Draconian**: Variable breath types
- **Pale Draconian**: Invisibility abilities

### Octopode
**Innate Traits**: 8 ring slots, amphibious, no footwear, tentacle attacks
**Aptitudes**: Spellcasting 110%, unarmed 100%, stealth 90%
**Strengths**: Best jewelry potential, water advantages, good HP
**Weaknesses**: Can't wear boots, medium land speed
**Recommended Backgrounds**: Elementalist, Summoner, Enchanter
**Progression**: Standard with excellent customization potential

### Gargoyle
**Innate Traits**: Stone form, high AC, flight, slow movement, poor magic
**Aptitudes**: Fighting 120%, armor 150%, magic 40-50%
**Strengths**: Extreme armor potential, flight, damage resistance
**Weaknesses**: Very slow, terrible spellcasting, vulnerable to shatter
**Recommended Backgrounds**: Fighter, Gladiator, Berserker
**Progression**: Slow but tanky progression

### Formicid
**Innate Traits**: Acid spit, antennae, good with armor, steady movement
**Aptitudes**: Fighting 110%, throwing 120%, magic 70-80%
**Strengths**: Acid damage, good equipment use, balanced stats
**Weaknesses**: No standout advantages, mediocre magic
**Recommended Backgrounds**: Fighter, Hunter, Gladiator
**Progression**: Reliable advancement

### Barachi
**Innate Traits**: Jumping ability, good with armor, hopping movement
**Aptitudes**: Fighting 110%, dodging 120%, magic 80%
**Strengths**: Mobility advantages, tactical jumping
**Weaknesses**: Unusual movement pattern, no magic specialty
**Recommended Backgrounds**: Fighter, Hunter, Transmuter
**Progression**: Standard with tactical options

### Djinni
**Innate Traits**: Flight, fire form, intelligence, equipment in smoke form
**Aptitudes**: Magic 120-130%, fighting 70-80%
**Strengths**: Flight, fire resistance, good spellcasting
**Weaknesses**: Weak HP, vulnerable in human form
**Recommended Backgrounds**: Elementalist, Summoner, Chaos Knight
**Progression**: Fast magical advancement

## Obsolete Species

### High Elf
**Status**: Removed in version 0.17, replaced by Deep Elf
**Historical Traits**: High intelligence, good magic aptitudes
**Reason for Removal**: Too similar to Deep Elf, redundant

### Sludge Elf
**Status**: Removed in version 0.12
**Historical Traits**: Poison resistance, balanced aptitudes  
**Reason for Removal**: Overlap with other species

### Hill Dwarf
**Status**: Removed in version 0.21, replaced by Mountain Dwarf
**Historical Traits**: High HP, good with armor
**Reason for Removal**: Redundant with Mountain Dwarf

### Halfling
**Status**: Removed in version 0.19
**Historical Traits**: Small size, good stealth
**Reason for Removal**: Overlap with Spriggan design space

### Centaur
**Status**: Removed in version 0.17
**Historical Traits**: Fast movement, hoof attacks
**Reason for Removal**: Balance issues with movement speed

## Species Selection Strategy

### For Beginners
1. **Minotaur Berserker**: High damage, straightforward gameplay
2. **Human Fighter**: Balanced, forgiving learning curve
3. **Deep Elf Elementalist**: Learn magic systems effectively

### For High Win Rates
1. **Minotaur**: Consistently strong melee builds
2. **Deep Elf**: Powerful spellcasters with good defenses
3. **Demonspawn**: Unpredictable but powerful mutations

### For Challenge Runs
1. **Felid**: No equipment, 9 lives but very fragile
2. **Spriggan**: Speed tactics but food management
3. **Troll**: Pure melee with restrictions

### For Specific Playstyles
- **Stealth**: Spriggan, Felid, Deep Elf
- **Tank**: Mountain Dwarf, Gargoyle, Troll
- **Speed**: Spriggan, Felid, Naga (in water)
- **Magic**: Deep Elf, Djinni, Demonspawn
- **Hybrid**: Human, Naga, Formicid

## Species-Specific Tips

### Spriggan
- Eat chunks immediately when hungry
- Use speed to escape dangerous situations
- Focus on stealth-based builds

### Troll
- Unarmed combat is surprisingly effective
- Berserk frequently for damage spikes
- Don't worry about equipment

### Minotaur
- Always use horns when available
- Excel with axes and heavy armor
- Berserk builds work well

### Deep Elf
- Prioritize magic skills early
- Use robe armor for spellcasting
- Avoid heavy melee combat early

### Naga
- Use water whenever possible
- Consider polearms for reach
- Learn constriction tactics

### Demonspawn
- Adapt to mutation-based abilities
- Flexible god selection
- Watch for chaotic mutation effects
