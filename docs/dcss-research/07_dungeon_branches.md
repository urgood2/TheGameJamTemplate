# DCSS Dungeon Branches Guide

This guide provides an overview of the various branches and levels in Dungeon Crawl Stone Soup (DCSS), including their depths, themes, and key features.

## The Main Dungeon

The central path of the game, consisting of 15 levels.
- **Depth**: 15 levels (D:1 to D:15).
- **Theme**: Varied, becoming more dangerous as you descend.
- **Key Features**: Entry point for most other branches.

---

## The Lair of Beasts

A natural, cave-like branch filled with animals and hydras.
- **Depth**: 5 levels (Lair:1 to Lair:5).
- **Entry**: Found in D:8-11.
- **Key Features**: Entry point for sub-branches (Swamp, Snake Pit, Shoals, Spider's Nest).

### Lair Sub-Branches
- **The Swamp**: Water-filled levels with hydras and dragons.
- **The Snake Pit**: Filled with nagas and salamanders.
- **The Shoals**: Aquatic levels with merfolk and sirens.
- **The Spider's Nest**: Filled with spiders and insects.

---

## Orcish Mines

A short branch filled with orcs and shops.
- **Depth**: 2 levels (Orc:1 to Orc:2).
- **Entry**: Found in D:9-12.
- **Key Features**: High concentration of gold and shops.

---

## Elven Halls

A dangerous branch filled with powerful elven mages.
- **Depth**: 3 levels (Elf:1 to Elf:3).
- **Entry**: Found in Orc:2.
- **Key Features**: High-level magical loot and artifacts.

---

## The Vaults

A heavily guarded branch filled with humanoids and treasure.
- **Depth**: 5 levels (Vaults:1 to Vaults:5).
- **Entry**: Found in D:13-14.
- **Key Features**: Contains the Silver Rune on Vaults:5.

---

## The Depths

The final stretch before the Realm of Zot.
- **Depth**: 4 levels (Depths:1 to Depths:4).
- **Entry**: Found in D:15.
- **Theme**: High-level monsters from all over the dungeon.

---

## The Realm of Zot

The final branch of the game, containing the Orb of Zot.
- **Depth**: 5 levels (Zot:1 to Zot:5).
- **Entry**: Found in Depths:4 (requires 3 runes).
- **Key Features**: Draconians, Orbs of Fire, and the Orb of Zot on Zot:5.

---

## Extended Game Branches

These branches are optional and contain additional runes for a "15-rune" win.

### The Abyss
A chaotic, shifting plane of infinite levels.
- **Key Features**: Contains the Abyssal Rune.

### Pandemonium
A series of unique levels ruled by demon lords.
- **Key Features**: Contains 5 different runes.

### The Hells
Four elemental branches (Cocytus, Gehenna, Tartarus, Dis).
- **Key Features**: Each contains a rune.

### The Slime Pits
A corrosive branch filled with slimes and jellies.
- **Key Features**: Contains the Slimy Rune.

---

## Portal Vaults

Special, temporary levels that appear randomly.
- **The Sewers**: Early game, water-themed.
- **The Ossuary**: Early game, undead-themed.
- **The Bailey**: Mid game, humanoid-themed.
- **The Gauntlet**: Combat arena with choices.
- **The Ice Cave**: Cold-themed.
- **The Volcano**: Fire-themed.
- **The WizLab**: High-level magical challenges.
- **The Ziggurat**: An extremely difficult, multi-level combat challenge.
