## Item System Overview

### Acquisition
- **Item Shop**: Appears every 3rd level (3, 6, 9, 12, 15, 18, 21, 24)
- **Selection**: Choose from 4 random items
- **Reroll Cost**: 5 gold (unlimited rerolls)
- **Capacity**: Maximum 8 items at once

### Item Leveling
| Level | XP Required | Total Cost |
|-------|-------------|------------|
| 1 → 2 | 2 XP | 10 gold (5 per XP) |
| 2 → 3 | 3 XP | 15 gold (5 per XP) |

**How to Level**: Click item in shop to spend 5 gold and add 1 XP

## Item Selection Tips

1. **Focus on 2-3 classes** - Don't spread items across too many classes
2. **Prioritize damage amplification early** - Amplify, Ballista, Call of the Void
3. **Get survivability mid-game** - Defensive Stance, Hardening, Divine Blessing
4. **Scale late-game** - Ultimatum, Unleash, Dividends
5. **Reroll aggressively** - 5 gold reroll is worth it for synergistic items
6. **Level key items to Lv. 3** - Assassination, Psychosink, Enchanted scale massively
7. **Watch for set bonuses** - Items that require class sets (Enchanted needs 2+ Enchanters)
