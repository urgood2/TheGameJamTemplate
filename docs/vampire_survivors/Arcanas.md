# Vampire Survivors: Arcanas Reference

## Overview

Arcanas are powerful run modifiers that significantly alter gameplay. The system is unlocked by finding the **Randomazzo** relic in Gallo Tower.

---

## Arcana System

### Unlocking Arcanas
1. Find **Randomazzo** relic in Gallo Tower (north of spawn)
2. Reach Level 50 with specific characters OR survive 31 minutes in stages
3. Collect **Darkasso** relic for Darkanas (9 additional cards)

### Arcana Selection
- Choose **1 Arcana** at run start
- Boss drops **Arcana Chest** at 11:00 (choose from 4 random)
- Boss drops **Arcana Chest** at 21:00 (choose from 4 random)
- Maximum **3 Arcanas** per run (standard)
- Queen Sigma can select extra Arcanas at levels 1/2/3/77/108

## Complete Arcana List (22 Cards)

### 0 - Game Killer
| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| **Unlock** | Defeat The Ender in Cappella Magna |
| **Effect** | Halts XP gain. XP gems become exploding projectiles. All Treasure Chests contain at least 3 items. |

**Strategy**: Late-game power spike. Use after you've maxed key weapons.

---

### I - Gemini
| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| **Unlock** | Reach Level 50 with Pugnala |
| **Effect** | Certain paired weapons come with a counterpart (Peachone/Ebony Wings, Phiera/Eight, etc.) |

**Synergies**: 
- Start with Peachone → Get Ebony Wings free
- Start with Phiera → Get Eight free
- Enables easy Vandalier/Phieraggi unions

---

### II - Twilight Requiem
| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| **Unlock** | Reach Level 50 with Dommario |
| **Effect** | King Bible, Lightning Ring, Peachone, Ebony Wings, Runetracer, Bone projectiles generate explosions when they expire. Explosion damage affected by Curse. |

**Synergies**:
- King Bible + Unholy Vespers = Constant explosions
- Lightning Ring + Thunder Loop = AoE chain explosions
- High Curse builds for more explosion damage

---

### III - Tragic Princess
| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| **Unlock** | Reach Level 50 with Porta |
| **Effect** | Cooldown of Garlic, Santa Water, Lightning Ring, and other listed weapons reduces when moving |

**Synergies**:
- High MoveSpeed builds
- Wings passive
- Constant movement playstyle

---

### IV - Awake
| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| **Unlock** | Reach Level 50 with Krochi |
| **Effect** | +3 Revivals. Consuming a Revival gives +10% Max Health, +1 Armor, +5% Might/Area/Duration/Speed |

**Strategy**: Excellent survivability. Die intentionally to stack permanent buffs.

**Synergies**:
- Tirajisú (+2 more Revivals)
- Krochi character (+1 Revival at start)
- Total 6+ Revivals possible

---

### V - Chaos in the Dark Night
| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| **Unlock** | Reach Level 50 with Giovanna |
| **Effect** | Overall projectile Speed continuously changes between -50% and +50% over 10 seconds. +1% projectile Speed every level. |

**Strategy**: Chaotic but scales well with levels.

---

### VI - Sarabande of Healing
| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| **Unlock** | Unlocked immediately after finding Randomazzo |
| **Effect** | Healing is doubled. Recovering HP damages nearby enemies for the same amount. |

**Synergies**:
- Bloody Tear (HP drain becomes damage)
- Soul Eater (kills heal and damage)
- Pummarola (constant healing = constant damage)
- High Recovery builds

---

### VII - Iron Blue Will
| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| **Unlock** | Reach Level 50 with Gennaro |
| **Effect** | Knife, Axe, Phiera Der Tuphello, Eight The Sparrow projectiles gain up to 3 bounces and might pass through enemies/walls |

**Synergies**:
- Thousand Edge (bouncing knives)
- Death Spiral (bouncing axes)
- Phieraggi (bouncing 8-directional fire)

---

### VIII - Mad Groove
| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| **Unlock** | Reach minute 31 in Mad Forest |
| **Effect** | Every 2 minutes attracts all standard stage items, pickups, and light sources toward the character |

**Strategy**: Automatic item collection. Essential for Boss Rash to collect distant items.

---

### IX - Divine Bloodline
| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| **Unlock** | Reach Level 50 with Clerici |
| **Effect** | Armor affects listed weapons' damage and reflects enemy damage. Bonus damage based on missing Health. Defeating enemies with retaliatory damage gives +0.5 Max Health. |

**Synergies**:
- High Armor builds
- Armor passive item
- Low HP playstyle for bonus damage

---

### X - Beginning
| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| **Unlock** | Reach Level 50 with Antonio |
| **Effect** | Listed weapons get +1 Amount. Character's main weapon and evolution gain +3 Amount instead. |

**Strategy**: Massive boost to starting weapon. Best with high-projectile weapons.

**Synergies**:
- Antonio (Whip → Bloody Tear with +3 Amount)
- Gennaro (Knife → Thousand Edge with +3 Amount)
- Any character with projectile-based starting weapon

---

### XI - Waltz of Pearls

**Synergies**:

---

### XII - Crystal Cries

|----------|-------|

### XV - Disco of Gold
| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| **Unlock** | Reach minute 31 in Inlaid Library |
| **Effect** | Picking up Gold heals 1 HP. Picking up Floor Chicken grants 10 Gold. |

**Synergies**:

---

### XVI - Slash
| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| **Unlock** | Reach Level 50 with Lama |
| **Effect** | Enables critical hits for Knife, Thousand Edge, Axe, Death Spiral, Phiera Der Tuphello, Eight The Sparrow, Vento Sacro, Fuwalafuwaloo, Victory Sword, and Muramasa. Doubles overall critical damage. |

**Strategy**: Essential for crit-based builds. Massive damage increase.

**Synergies**:
- Clover (more Luck = more crits)
- Heaven Sword (already crits, now 2x damage)
- Phieraggi (8-directional crits)

---

### XVII - Lost & Found Painting
| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| **Unlock** | Reach minute 31 in Dairy Plant |
| **Effect** | Replaces Arcana Chests with up to 4 standard stage items |

**Strategy**: Trade Arcanas for more passive items.

---

### XVIII - Boogaloo of Illusions
| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| **Unlock** | Reach minute 31 in Cappella Magna |
| **Effect** | Replaces Arcana Chests with up to 4 random weapons |

**Strategy**: Trade Arcanas for more weapons.

---

### XIX - Heart of Fire
| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| **Unlock** | Reach Level 50 with Arca |
| **Effect** | Halves enemy health. Halves enemy speed. Doubles enemy count. |

**Strategy**: More enemies = more XP, but they're easier to kill.

**Synergies**:
- AoE weapons (more targets)
- Crown (more XP from doubled enemies)
- Attractorb (collect more gems)

---

### XX - Silent Old Sanctuary
| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| **Unlock** | Reach minute 31 in Holy Forbidden |
| **Effect** | Halves enemy speed. Doubles enemy health. |

**Strategy**: Slower but tankier enemies. Good for kiting builds.

---

### XXI - Blood Astronomia
| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| **Unlock** | Reach Level 50 with Poe |
| **Effect** | Halves character's Max Health. Doubles character's Might, Projectile Speed, Duration, and Area. |

**Strategy**: Glass cannon build. High risk, high reward.

**Synergies**:
- Tirajisú (Revivals offset low HP)
- Awake (Revivals give permanent buffs)
- High damage weapons

## Arcana Tier List

### S-Tier (Game-Changing)
- **XVI - Slash**: Enables crits, doubles crit damage
- **IV - Awake**: +3 Revivals with permanent stat gains
- **I - Gemini**: Free paired weapons
- **VI - Sarabande of Healing**: Healing becomes damage

### A-Tier (Excellent)
- **X - Beginning**: +3 Amount to starting weapon
- **II - Twilight Requiem**: Explosions on projectile expiry
- **VII - Iron Blue Will**: Bouncing projectiles
- **XIX - Heart of Fire**: More enemies, easier kills

### B-Tier (Good)
- **VIII - Mad Groove**: Auto-collect items
- **IX - Divine Bloodline**: Armor-based damage
- **XXI - Blood Astronomia**: Glass cannon stats
- **XI - Waltz of Pearls**: Bouncing projectiles

### C-Tier (Situational)
- **XII - Crystal Cries**: More rerolls/banishes
- **XV - Disco of Gold**: Gold healing
- **III - Tragic Princess**: Movement-based cooldown
- **XIV - Jail of Crystal**: Duration bonus

### D-Tier (Niche)
- **0 - Game Killer**: XP gems become projectiles
- **V - Chaos in the Dark Night**: Random speed changes
- **XIII - Wicked Season**: Area bonus with cooldown penalty
- **XVII/XVIII - Lost & Found/Boogaloo**: Trade Arcanas for items/weapons
- **XX - Silent Old Sanctuary**: Slower, tankier enemies
