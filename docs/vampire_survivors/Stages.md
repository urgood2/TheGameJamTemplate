# Vampire Survivors: Stages Reference

## Overview

Vampire Survivors features **54 official stages** across the base game and DLCs:
- 20 base game stages
- 7+ DLC stages
- 27 adventure mode-exclusive stages

Each stage has unique layouts, enemies, bosses, and hidden items.

---

## Stage Mechanics

### Time Limits
| Stage Type | Time Limit | Reaper Spawn |
|------------|------------|--------------|
| Standard | 30:00 | 30:00+ |
| Short | 15:00 | 15:00+ |
| Medium | 20:00 | 20:00+ |
| Special | 2:00 | N/A |

### Stage Modifiers
- **Normal Mode**: Base difficulty
- **Hyper Mode**: +65-100% MoveSpeed, +50% Gold, increased enemy stats
- **Hurry Mode**: 2x clock speed (15 min = 30 min)
- **Inverse Mode**: +200% Gold, +20% Luck, +200% Enemy HP, stage rotated 180°
- **Endless Mode**: No Reaper, waves restart with scaling difficulty

### Special Stages (2)

#### Holy Forbidden
| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| **Unlock** | Survive 14 min in Moongolow, defeat special boss |
| **Time Limit** | 2:00 |
| **Modifiers** | +25% MoveSpeed |

**Stage Effects**:
- Player loses most upgrades (Revivals, Armor, MoveSpeed, Growth, Arcana, items, weapons)
- Keeps only starting weapon
- Game cannot be paused
- Must navigate corridor, pick up Rosary to banish enemies

**Relic**: Yellow Sign (reveals hidden items on all stages)

**Special**: Permanently inaccessible after obtaining Yellow Sign

## DLC Stages

### Legacy of the Moonspell
#### Mt. Moonspell
- **Hyper Modifiers**: +100% MoveSpeed, +10% Luck, +100% Gold
- Japanese-themed mountain stage
- New enemy types and bosses

### Tides of the Foscari
#### Lake Foscari
- Water-themed stage
- New enemy types

#### Abyss Foscari
- Deep underwater stage
- Challenging enemy patterns

### Emergency Meeting
#### Polus Replica
- Among Us-themed stage
- Impostor enemies

### Operation Guns
#### Neo Galuga
- Contra-themed stage
- Shooter enemy patterns

#### Hectic Highway
- Vehicle-based stage
- Reaper spawns at 15:00+

### Ode to Castlevania
#### Dracula's Castle
- Castlevania-themed stage
- Multiple areas and bosses
- Teleporters, Heart Refreshes, Karma Coins
- Unlock by defeating specific bosses
