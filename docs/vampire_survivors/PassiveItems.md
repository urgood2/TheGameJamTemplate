# Vampire Survivors: Passive Items Reference

## Overview

Passive items provide permanent stat bonuses during a run. Each item has 5 levels (some have 2), and specific items are required for weapon evolutions.

**Limits**: 
- 6 passive items can be selected from level-ups
- Additional passives can be found as floor pickups on stages

## Passive Item Tier List

### S-Tier (Always Pick)
- **Spinach** - Universal damage
- **Empty Tome** - More attacks
- **Duplicator** - Extra projectiles
- **Crown** - Faster leveling
- **Attractorb** - Easier XP collection

### A-Tier (Usually Pick)
- **Candelabrador** - Larger hitboxes
- **Spellbinder** - Duration for orbitals
- **Hollow Heart** - Double HP
- **Tirajisú** - Extra lives

### B-Tier (Situational)
- **Bracer** - For projectile builds
- **Wings** - Mobility
- **Armor** - Damage reduction
- **Pummarola** - Sustain
- **Clover** - Luck builds

### C-Tier (Specific Builds)
- **Stone Mask** - Gold farming
- **Skull O'Maniac** - Curse builds
- **Silver/Gold Ring** - Infinite Corridor
- **Metaglios** - Crimson Shroud
- **Torrona's Box** - Sole Solution

## Passive Item Synergies

### DPS Build
- Spinach (+50% Might)
- Candelabrador (+50% Area)
- Empty Tome (-40% Cooldown)
- Duplicator (+2 Amount)
- **Result**: Maximum damage output

### Survival Build
- Hollow Heart (+100% Max HP)
- Pummarola (+1 Recovery/sec)
- Armor (+5 Armor)
- Tirajisú (+2 Revivals)
- **Result**: Very hard to kill

### Farming Build
- Crown (+40% Growth)
- Attractorb (+250% Magnet)
- Stone Mask (+50% Greed)
- Clover (+50% Luck)
- **Result**: Fast leveling, more gold

### Curse Build
- Skull O'Maniac (+50% Curse)
- Silver Ring (+50% Curse)
- Gold Ring (+50% Curse)
- Metaglio Right (+25% Curse)
- **Result**: +175% Curse, massive enemy spawns
