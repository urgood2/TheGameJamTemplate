## Overview

Magicraft features **100 unique attribute staffs** that serve as the primary weapon system. Each staff provides different base stats, special abilities, and modification slots that define the player's magical capabilities and build potential.

## Modification System

### **Modifier Types**
Staffs can be enhanced with various modifiers:

| Modifier Type | Effect | Rarity | Cost |
|---------------|--------|--------|------|
| **Power Crystals** | Increase spell power | Common | Low |
| **Speed Runes** | Increase cast speed | Common | Low |
| **Efficiency Gems** | Reduce mana cost | Uncommon | Medium |
| **Elemental Stones** | Boost elemental damage | Rare | High |
| **Master Orbs** | Add special abilities | Legendary | Very High |

### **Modification Slots**
- **1 Slot**: Basic staffs, limited customization
- **2 Slots**: Standard staffs, moderate customization
- **3 Slots**: Advanced staffs, good customization
- **4 Slots**: Master staffs, full customization

### **Modification Rules**
- **Compatibility**: Not all modifiers work with all staffs
- **Stacking**: Same type modifiers have diminishing returns
- **Synergy**: Certain modifier combinations create bonus effects
- **Rarity**: Higher rarity modifiers provide better effects

### **Upgrade System**
- **Leveling**: Staffs gain experience and improve stats
- **Enhancement**: Use resources to upgrade staff attributes
- **Evolution**: Certain staffs can evolve into more powerful forms
- **Awakening**: Unlock hidden potential in master staffs

### **Mastery System**
- **Staff Mastery**: Repeated use with same staff type
- **Elemental Mastery**: Specialize in specific elements
- **Combination Mastery**: Enhanced effects with specific spell combinations
- **Style Mastery**: Bonuses for preferred playstyles

## Balance Analysis

### **Overpowered Staffs**
- **Void Staff**: Ignores all defense with high damage
- **Archmage Staff**: Universal elemental bonuses
- **Infinity Staff**: No mana cost with good stats

### **Underutilized Staffs**
- **Basic Staffs**: Outclassed by specialized options
- **Single-Element Staffs**: Limited compared to hybrid options
- **Defensive Staffs**: Lower damage output in fast-paced combat

### **Meta Considerations**
- **Speed vs Power**: Fast casting vs high damage tradeoff
- **Specialization vs Versatility**: Focused vs adaptable builds
- **Rarity vs Accessibility**: Legendary staffs vs common options
