# Path of Achra: Items Reference

## Overview

Items in Path of Achra provide stats, resistances, and **triggered abilities**. Every class starts with initial equipment, and more items are found throughout areas.

### Equipment Slots
- **Main-hand** (weapon/shield)
- **Off-hand** (weapon/shield)
- **Head** (armor)
- **Arm** (armor)
- **Chest** (armor)
- **Leg** (armor)

### Key Stats
- **Accuracy** - Hit chance
- **Hit** - Damage dealt
- **Type** - Damage type (Slash, Blunt, Pierce, elemental)
- **Block** - Block value
- **Range** - Attack range (1 = melee)
- **Encumbrance** - Weight (affects Speed/mobility)
- **Weapon Size** - Determines two-handing requirements
- **Armor** - Damage reduction
- **Inflexibility** - Movement penalty

## Design Patterns

### Damage Type Conversion
Many items convert primary damage type to secondary:
- **50% conversion**: Azar Axe (Slash->Fire), Storm Lance (Pierce->Lightning), etc.
- **25% conversion**: All sashes convert elemental damage

### Status Effect Application
Items commonly apply effects on:
- **On attack**: Noxious Dagger (Sickness), Bone Knife (Bleed)
- **On block/being hit**: Bone Shield (Bleed), Syre Shield (Corrosion)
- **On Physical damage**: Bone Pakarna (Bleed), Emerald Gauntlets (Sickness)

## Implementation Priority for Survivors-Like

### High Priority
1. **Damage Conversion** - Simple % conversion from one type to another
2. **On-Hit Effects** - Apply status on hit (Bleed, Sickness, etc.)
3. **Stat Scaling Weapons** - Damage scales with Speed, STR, DEX, WIL
4. **Extra Attack Triggers** - On initial attack, on dodge, on step

### Medium Priority
1. **Block/Defense Triggers** - Counterattack on block or being hit
2. **Effect Stack Scaling** - Zengja, Jade items that scale with buffs
3. **Summoning Items** - Items that summon minions
4. **Self-Damage Items** - Risk/reward mechanics

### Lower Priority
1. **Two-Handing Requirements** - Equipment combination rules
2. **Empty Armor Synergies** - Naked/light armor builds
3. **Transformation Items** - Equipment that changes other equipment
4. **Stand Still Items** - Stationary turret builds
